About Us

Who we are ?

Smart Strategies for Successful Living, Inc. is a nonprofit (501c3) charitable foundation that offers a thought-provoking and innovative look at how to live life to the fullest. Its unique community-based website and FREE online newsletters are designed to provide a venue for people to exchange ideas on how to understand, embrace, and enhance their individual pathways for successful living. It includes articles and shared tips by community members and professionals on a variety of enjoyable and relevant subjects that focus on the personal growth and well-being of women and men of all ages.
